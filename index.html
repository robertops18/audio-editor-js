Freesound Editor Help

Welcome to the Freesound online audio editor! These are the available operations:

  • General operations
    • Play and pause (Spacebar)
    • Skip backward (Left arrow) and forward (Right arrow)
    • Undo (Ctrl or Cmd + Z) and redo (Ctrl or Cmd + Shift + Z)
    • Download edited sample
  • Waveform related actions
    • Zoom to selected region
    • Delete selected region (Backspace)
    • Clear selected region
    • Get original sample
    • Reset filters
    • Reverse sample
    • Zoom in and out
  • Filters
    • Lowpass filter
    • Highpass filter
    • Bandpass filter
    • Lowshelf filter
    • Highshelf filter
    • Peaking filter
    • Notch filter
    • Allpass filter
  • Effects
    • Amplify waveform
    • Fade in and out (select the point to start and the duration of the effect and then apply)
    • Bitcrush effect
